Ingredients of Aloo Paneer Masala
  • 11/2 tablespoon ghee

  • 1 piece cinnamon stick

  • chopped coriander leaves as required

  • 2 1/4 teaspoon garlic paste

  • 2 teaspoon coriander powder

  • 2 medium pureed tomato

  • 2 medium chopped into cubes potato

  • 1/4 cup onion paste

  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

  • 1 bay leaf

  • 2 1/4 teaspoon ginger paste

  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric

  • 1 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder

  • salt as required

  • 250 gm chopped into cubes paneer


How to make Aloo Paneer Masala
      Step 1 Prepare the masala
    • Heat ghee in a pan/ kadhai, add cumin seeds, bay leaf, and cinnamon stick. Cook for 1 minute and then add onion paste. Cook for 2 more minutes and then add ginger and garlic paste. Cook on medium flame till the liquid dries out.
      Step 2 Add whole spices
    • Now, to the mixture, add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and 1 tbsp water. Stir to mix well and Cook for 3 mins. Add tomato puree and cook till oil leaves the masala
      Step 3 Stir in potatoes
    • Add potatoes and then season with salt. Mix it well and then add 3 cups of water. Cover the lid and then bring it to a boil. Once the potatoes are cooked, add paneer cubes and cook for 5 more minutes. Then turn off the gas flame.
      Step 4 Garnish it with coriander leaves
    • Your Aloo Paneer masala is ready to serve. Garnish it with some freshly chopped coriander leaves. Enjoy the meal.
