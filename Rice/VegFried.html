Ingredients of Veg Fried Rice
  • 250 gm chopped into cubes paneer

  • 1 1/2 tablespoon corn flour

  • 2 dry red chili

  • 1 medium chopped capsicum (green pepper)

  • 1 handful chopped spring onions

  • 1/4 cup crushed peanuts

  • gm salt

  • water as required

  • 1 1/2 tablespoon red chilli sauce

  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil

  • 1 1/2 teaspoon chopped garlic

  • 1 medium thinly sliced onion

  • 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce

  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder

  • gm black pepper


How to make Veg Fried Rice
      Step 1 Chop the veggies
    • Firstly, chop all the veggies like onion, carrot, capsicum, cauliflower, baby corn, beans into small pieces and set them aside. Also, chop the paneer into small cubes.
      Step 2 The prepration
    • Heat oil in a pan. Add asafoetida, cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them splutter for a minute. Now add chopped onion and saute them for a few minutes until they turn golden. Now add all the veggies like carrot, capsicum, cauliflower, baby corn, beans and peas.
      Step 3 Add the spices
    • Add salt, red chilli powder, dry mango powder, coriander powder, turmeric and give a nice mix. Now add 1/4 cup water. This will prevent the veggies from becoming too dry and will produce steam which will quickly cook the veggies. Cover the pan with a lid for 6-8 minutes.
      Step 4 Add paneer
    • Now open the lid, add paneer cubes, garam masala and give a gentle mix. Cook for another 5 minutes and switch off the flame.
